Jure Leskovec is an associate professor of Computer Science at Stanford University focusing on networks. He is the chief scientist at Pinterest.[4]\n

\n

Biography[edit]

\n

In 2004, Leskovec received a Diploma in Computer Science from the University of Ljubljana, Slovenia, researching semantic networks-based creation of abstracts, using machine learning; in 2008 he received a PhD in Computational and Statistical Learning from the Carnegie Mellon University.\n
