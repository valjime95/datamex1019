Welcome to the File Upload Wizard. This page is for uploading images and other media files to Wikipedia. When you click the link below, the wizard will guide you through a questionnaire prompting you for the appropriate copyright and sourcing information for each file. Please ensure you understand copyright and the image use policy before proceeding.\n

Click here to start the Upload Wizard

Your account has not become confirmed yet.\n

Sorry, in order to upload files on the English Wikipedia, you need to have a confirmed account. Normally, your account will become confirmed automatically once you have made 10 edits and four days have passed since you created it. \n

You may already be able to upload files on the Wikimedia Commons, but you can\'t do it on the English Wikipedia just yet. If the file you want to upload has a free license, please go to Commons and upload it there.\n

Important note: if you don\'t want to wait until you are autoconfirmed, you may ask somebody else to upload a file for you at Wikipedia:Files for upload. \n

In very rare cases an administrator may make your account confirmed manually through a request at Wikipedia:Requests for permissions/Confirmed.\n

Step 2: Describe your file

\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n
Please provide a clear, descriptive name by which your file will be known on Wikipedia.\n
 \n
This name must be unique across the whole of Wikipedia, so please make it informative and easy to recognize.
It\'s no problem to use a fairly long name. It may also include spaces, commas and most other punctuation marks.
Please also note that file names are case sensitive (with the exception of the first letter).
Good: "City of London, skyline from London City Hall, Oct 2008.jpg". Bad: "Skyline.jpg", "DSC0001234.jpg".
